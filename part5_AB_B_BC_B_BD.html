Crossing the bridge

The Rules

Four people come to a river in the night. There is a narrow bridge, but it can only hold two people at a time.

They have one torch and, because it’s night, the torch has to be used when crossing the bridge. The torch only lasts 15 minutes before going out.

The archer can cross the bridge in 1 minute, the knight in 2 minutes, the ogre in 5 minutes, and the king in 8 minutes.

When people cross the bridge together, they must move at the lower person’s pace. The question is, can they all get across the bridge in 15 minutes or less ?
